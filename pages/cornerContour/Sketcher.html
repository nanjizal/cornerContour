@:value({ sides : 24 })inlinecircle(radius:Float, sides:Float = 24):Sketcher

circle

Draw a circle with a given radius. The center is radius units left of the turtle if positive. Otherwise radius units right of the turtle if negative. The circle is drawn in an anticlockwise direction if the radius is positive, otherwise, it is drawn in a clockwise direction.
